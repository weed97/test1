# Magic System

Magic is real, respected and feared. It is **costly, finite and never free**.

## Mana
- Spellcasters draw on **mana**, a personal reserve set by Intelligence and Wisdom.
- Every spell has a **mana cost**. When mana runs out, no more spells — fall back on
  steel, wits or flight. Mana regenerates slowly with rest, faster at a font.

## Schools
- **Evocation** — fire, frost, lightning. Raw damage. (firebolt, frost lance, meteor)
- **Restoration** — healing and mending. (heal, greater heal)
- **Abjuration** — wards and shields. (arcane shield, ice armor)
- **Conjuration** — summon matter/venom. (poison dart)
- **Holy** — divine light, anathema to undead and demons. (holy smite, bless)

## Hard limits (enforce these)
1. No instant kills of named characters by a single spell. Powerful magic is dramatic
   but resolvable.
2. No resurrection of the truly dead, no time travel, no mind control of player
   characters.
3. Elemental logic matters: fire melts frost-things; holy burns the undead; constructs
   resist poison entirely.
4. Casting in a city without cause draws the Crown's and Conclave's attention.

## Adjudication
A spell's effect is gated by a **dice check** when contested (see `combat_rules.md`).
Describe the *crackle and cost* of magic, not just the result. Spell power scales with
the caster's Intelligence/Wisdom and level.
